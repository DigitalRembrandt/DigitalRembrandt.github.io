Frequently asked questions

Is Solitaire Elite free?

Please check the App Store listing for current pricing. We may offer a free version, a paid version, or optional in-app purchases. Check the listing for the most up-to-date information.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. Solitaire Elite works fully offline. You can play on a plane, subway, or anywhere without Wi-Fi or cellular data.

How do I reset my scores or game history?

Go to your device's Settings → General → iPhone Storage, find Solitaire Elite, and tap "Offload App" or delete and reinstall it. This will clear all locally stored data.

My game progress was lost after reinstalling. Why?

Game data is stored locally on your device and is not backed up to the cloud. Deleting or reinstalling the app will reset all progress. iCloud backup may preserve some data depending on your device settings.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Use the contact form below. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a description of what happened. We read every message.
